Ingredients for Pasta:

1. 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or any type of choice)
2. 2 tablespoons olive oil
3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
4. 1/2 onion, finely chopped
5. 1 cup canned tomatoes or fresh diced tomatoes
6. 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
7. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
8. 1 teaspoon dried oregano or Italian seasoning
9. 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)
10. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
11. Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
12. 1/2 cup cooked vegetables (mushrooms, bell peppers, or zucchini, optional)
13. 1/2 cup cooked protein (chicken, tofu, or shrimp, optional)
14. 1/2 cup heavy cream or milk (for creamy pasta, optional)

Instructions:
1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and onions, and saut√© until fragrant.
3. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper, oregano, and chili flakes. Cook until sauce thickens.
4. Mix in cooked vegetables or protein if using.
5. Add cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing well to coat.
6. Stir in cheese and mix until melted.
7. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.
